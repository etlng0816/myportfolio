Welcome to Whispers of Words

I possess a boundless imagination that allows me to dream of a world with no wars, no poverty, and plenty of harmony and mutual support. Imagination for me is not flights of fancy; it's the connecting device between what is and what could be. Seeing communities bonded through a sense of purpose, each person contributing their piece of the tapestry that is progressing with compassion is what I often dream about. Anything but a wishful daydream, it is more of a wake-up call to the rude realities of today's world. It stirs in me an ardor, fosters change, and whets my determination to make this imagination real. A compass, it guides me toward new paths, and yet, at the same time, it can become a catalyst to help me let others be inspired in the same journey. This is the prism of endless possibility from which I draw the strength to forge ahead in the work of translating ideals into action and dreams into reality.

Mother's Love

What is the sky where there are no stars?

What is the sky where there are no stars? What is the sea where there are no fishes? What we can’t touch though we know it’s not far? What we can’t see though we know it exists? What we can just feel and receive it from its only owner?—our greatest mother. It’s something that can’t be defined Something that can’t be derived from us Something that cannot be judged As it’s precious Than any other things. It comes from where all the lives start And won’t disappear even if the lives end It’s the sun helping us overcome things that are harsh And guides us whenever we lose our directions. It’s mother’s love which is higher than sky and larger than sea which we can feel or receive but cannot touch or see. It’s mother’s love.

Another challenging year rolled by

Another challenging year rolled by, There were people exhausted, and there were people died... Another fight with an unknown ending, A fight against issues that are nagging. Another year witnessed many an event, From the cry for help of Mother Earth, To the impotent tears of youths from Afghanistan. But as it's usually said everything has both sides, 2021, for sure, is not besides... It was the year when sportsmanship surpassed the pandemic, It was the year of the first commercial flight into the space, And the opening of a new era for mankind. It was also the year when all limits were broken, with technology. That's why we have the right to believe in a brighter 2022, When wars and epidemics will be eradicated, When the world is only the world of peace and joy.

2025

Today is the first day of the rest of my life

Today is the first day of the rest of my life under the past, I draw a line no longer weep or dwell on cry my future beckons with hopes so rife. The highs and lows, they’ve sapped my might, out of me, they’ve scared a daylight But courage sparks in the dead of night Trials faced, endless fight, and my path alight. Soon I realized nothing ventured, nothing gained without the leap, the dream’s constrained Having transcended pain, I opened the door to the endeavors that I had long wooed. I give a crap about moments that last, not the crap of regrets that belong to the past Turn the page, guide the sage to ring in the new and ring out the aged. Like a lighthouse, I flashily dressed take in a breath of air that’s fresh with every step, my spirit’s blessed A change, it is, as good as a rest. Be there with bells on, I’ll laugh and shine through ups and downs, this path is mine with every challenge, I’ll rise and strive, so I wreathe my pride in gold for the rest of my life.

Kismet

A glance, a plan, an orchestrated chance

My one and only In thy embrace, I long to be. Thy words of endearment Instilled in me a belief fervent that me and thee, certain is a match made in heaven. Love makes my world go round in ways I cannot count But also it helps me surmount the hurdles scattered around. Oh! My type on paper I see thou fear the future But my dearest lover, Absence makes our hearts grow fonder. Oh! The love of my life The one sets my heart alight My only ride-or-die In thy touch, I’m forever tied.
